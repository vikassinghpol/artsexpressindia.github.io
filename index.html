Arts express Services

Warehousing and Storage

We have buildings that can provide you with distribution, storage, inventory management, order fulfilment, cross-docking, tranloading and other value added services. Our warehousing space gives you the ability to grow your footprint in a shared environment to minimize costs. We specilize in the USA, NETHERLANDS and UAE.

Air Freight

Arts express partners with airlines and freight forwarders to offer seamless air freight solutions to clients globally. We offer a competitive and quick service which includes our air express, high priority (24 hours) delivery, and value services.

Ocean Freight

Wherever your cargo is going, we can arrange the shipment for you and remain competitive with the market. Our ocean freight service volumes provide us buying power with major carriers. We can ship door to door and provide customs clearance.

Land Freight

We work close with strategic partners to ensure our domestic trucking offers clients everything they need. We utilize track and trace capabilities to keep customers informed on their shipments at all times. We offer reliable door service and have the ability to route, manage and schedule all shipments with our global comprehensive planning tool.

Project Logistics

Need to get a project done, but not sure where to start? No problem. Arts express can handle your logistics project from A - Z. Our teams across the globe are skilled at servicing all levels of the supply chain. Give us the details of your product's need and a timeline and we create a solution just for you!

E-Commerce Fulfillment

EFulfillment is on the rise more than ever. While many warehouses can do it, not many can do it right! We specialize in e-commerce work and have highly trained experience teams. We even over FBA Prep services as an alternative to Fulfilment By Amazon for clients.
